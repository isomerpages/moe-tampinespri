<h2><strong>2025 MK Registration for Sibling Priority Admission (Priority Order 2)</strong></h2>
<p>Dear Parents</p>
<p>Singapore Citizen (SC) and Permanent Resident (PR) children are eligible
for sibling priority during the 2025 MOE Kindergarten (MK) Registration
Exercise to 2026 Kindergarten 1 (K1) admission if:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>They are born between <strong>2 January 2021 and 1 January 2022 (both dates inclusive)</strong>;
and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have an elder sibling currently studying in K1 - K2 in the MK which the
child is registering, or P1 - P6 in the primary school that the MK is located
within.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>MK registration period is from <strong>9am on Friday, 7 February 2025 to 4pm on Monday, 17 February 2025</strong>.</p>
<p>Please refer to the attached letter for further details regarding the
registration.</p>
<p>Thank you.</p>
<p>Tampines Primary School</p>
