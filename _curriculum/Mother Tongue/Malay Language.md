<h1><strong>Malay Language</strong></h1>
<h3><strong>Malay Language Department</strong></h3>
<p>At Tampines Primary School, we envision every Tampinesian to be empowered
as proficient, confident and creative users of the Malay language. Grounded
in strong moral values and a deep sense of national identity, our learners
embody the spirit of Arif Budiman — individuals who are knowledgeable,
cultured and responsible, contributing meaningfully to society and the
nation.</p>
